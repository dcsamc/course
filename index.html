Programmes & Course Structure

Programmes offered


Department of Computere Science & Applications offers two undergraduate 3 years' self financed programmes, namely:

B.Sc in Computer Science (H) (Intake: 37)

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) (Intake: 49)



Course Structure


B.Sc. in Computer Science is a 3 years' programme. It follows the annual exam system.
BCA is a 3 years' programme. It follows the semester system.
